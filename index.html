Biography

I am a Ph.D student in Information Systems department of New Jersey Institute of Technology. Recently, I am working with Prof. Yi Chen.

My research interests are broadly in Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Text Mining, and Computational Advertising. The topic of my dissertation project is Viewability Prediction for Display Advertising.
